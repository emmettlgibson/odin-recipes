Tofu, Broccoli, and Rice

Description

This is a simple recipe that can be done in around an hour and even quicker if you do a little preparation beforehand. It is also a great option for meal prep.

Ingredients

This recipe only calls for three main ingredients, the rest will be seasonings.

  • Tofu
  • Broccoli
  • Rice

Seasonings

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Steak seasoning
  • Not chicken cubes
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Flour
  • Oil (Olive, grapeseed, etc)

Steps

Tofu

  1. Put three tofu packages in the freezer and let sit until frozen.
  2. Once frozen, taken out the freezer and let sit until thawed.
    • This is important as it will help the tofu be drained easier.
  3. When thawed out, open tofu and squeeze out water.
  4. Cut tofu into cubes and put into a large bowl
  5. Throw in oil then seasonings.
  6. Mix
  7. Throw in flour to coat tofu and hold seasonings.
  8. Put on a pan then put into the oven on 425.
  9. Cook for 40 minutes, flipping the tofu halfway through.
