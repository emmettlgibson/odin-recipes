Tofu, Broccoli, and Rice

Description

This is a simple recipe that can be done in around an hour and even quicker if you do a little preparation beforehand. It is also a great option for meal prep.

Ingredients

This recipe only calls for three main ingredients, the rest will be seasonings.

  • Tofu
  • Broccoli
  • Rice

Seasonings

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Steak seasoning
  • Not chicken cubes
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Flour
  • Soy sauce
  • Oil (Olive, grapeseed, etc)

Steps

Tofu

Needed

  • Three packages of 16oz super firm tofu
  • 1/4 cup olive or grapeseed oil
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 TBSP black pepper
  • 1 TBSP steak seasoning
  • 1 TBSP garlic powder
  • 1 TBSP onion powder

Cook

  1. Put three tofu packages in the freezer and let sit until frozen.
  2. Once frozen, taken out the freezer and let sit until thawed.
    • This is important as it will help the tofu be drained easier.
  3. When thawed out, open tofu and squeeze out water.
  4. Cut tofu into cubes and put into a large bowl.
  5. Throw in oil then seasonings.
  6. Mix.
  7. Throw in flour to coat tofu and hold seasonings.
  8. Put on a pan then put into the oven on 425.
  9. Cook for 40 minutes, flipping the tofu halfway through.
  10. After 40 minutes the tofu should be cooked.

Broccoli

Needed

  • Broccoli
  1. Put seven servings of broccoli into a pot.
  2. Put a cup of water into the pot.
  3. Put heat on high and cook until broccoli is at desired texture.

Rice

Needed

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 TBSP butter
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1 not chicken cube
  1. Portion two cups of rice and put into a strainer
  2. Rinse until the water from the strainer is clear.
    • This is important as it gets the startch off of the rice for a better cook.
  3. Put 1 TBSP of butter into a small sauce pan and turn heat on max
  4. Once the butter is melted put the rinsed rice.
  5. Put the seasonings into the rice and mix.
  6. Put four cups of water into the pot
  7. Bring to a boil
  8. Once at a boil immediately turn the temperature to the lowest heat.
  9. Let sit and do not take off the lid.
  10. Wait for 10-15 minutes and the rice should be done.

Portion

  1. Grab seven bowls that are air tight.
  2. Portion out the tofu, broccoli, and rice to each bowl.
