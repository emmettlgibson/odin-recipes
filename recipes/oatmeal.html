Meal Prep Oats

Description

This recipe for oatmeal is vegan and perfect for bulking. This recipe has 1120 calories and 39 grams of protein.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Dates
  • Flax seed
  • Cinnamon
  • Soy milk
  • Peanut butter
  • Walnuts

Needed

  • 1 cup oats
  • 3 pitted dates
  • 2 TBSP flax seed
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 cup soy milk
  • 4 TBSP peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup walnuts

Steps

  1. Put one cup of oats into a bowl.
  2. Cut up three dates into desired size and put them with the oats.
  3. Put in flax seed and cinnamon.
  4. Mix.
  5. Put one cup of soy milk into bowl and make sure is mixed thoroughly as well.
    • This is important as milk will need to cook through all the oats.
  6. Put mixture into the microwave for 4 minutes and let sit for the same amount of time.
  7. Take out of microwave and then put 4 TBSPs of peanut butter in to the bowl and mix.
    • This allows the peanut butter to melt nicely.
  8. Put 1/4 cup walnuts into the bowl.
