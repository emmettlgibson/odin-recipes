Vegan Protein Shake

Description

This chocolate protein shake is vegan and extremely simple.

Ingredients

  • Protein powder
  • Powdered peanut butter
  • Soy milk

Needed

  • 1 serving protein powder
  • 1 serving powdered peanut butter
  • Two cups soy milk

Steps

  1. Pour two cups of soy milk into shaker bottle.
  2. Put servings of protein powder and powdered protein into shaker bottle.
  3. Shake until the protein powder and powedered peanut butter is mixed.
